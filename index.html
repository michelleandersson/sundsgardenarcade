Welcome to Sundsgårdens Arcade

The Why

Each year, the participants in Sundgården Folkhögskola's IT courses finish off the course by creating a game from scratch in React. Unfortunately, since these games only live in the participants' local repositories, no one ever gets to play these games. We want to change that!

Now all the participants who want to may choose to show off their game on this website! Below you will find the games made by the different groups of participants throughout different years of studies at Sundsgården Folkhögskola.

Want to play a game?

Simple! Just scroll through the games below until you find one that you are interested in. Click on the image of the game and you will be take to the game's website where you can play!
